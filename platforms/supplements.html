Dietary Supplements._

Research Blocks provides dietary supplement manufacturers a simple-to-use software platform designed to reduce errors and make regulatory compliance easy. By utilizing a blockchain ledger system, each step along the production process is monitored and audited in real-time. Inventory, production steps, and quality checks are streamlined and easily documented allowing manufacturers to efficiently run their production line.

Finally, once a supplement is complete, a QR code is placed on the finished product. Customers can scan this QR code and determine the source and quality of the ingredients, an overview of the manufacturing process, and the quality checks implemented. By using Research Blocks’ software, not only will manufacturers increase efficiency, but consumers will also know which brands to trust.
