For Consumers._

Consumers, like you, are demanding to know where their consumables come from, how they are made, and the quality of ingredients used. It is well known that the FDA is not authorized to review dietary supplements before they go to market. This means dietary supplements are safe until proven to be unsafe and the general population is used as guinea pigs. Furthermore, CBD oil is becoming highly prevalent and used to treat a wide range of ailments such as chronic pain, anxiety, and inflammation. While CBD oil is becoming more available, the FDA is still deciding how to regulate this new product and it is hard to distinguish the good brands from the bad. Additionally, consumers are looking for ingredients in their food that they can trust and are excluding ingredients such as preservatives, artificial sweeteners, and unneeded chemical additives.

With the lack of transparency in the dietary supplements, CBD oil, and food markets, how can consumers know which consumables and supplements to trust when they are all labeled as quality and trustworthy products?

At Research Blocks, we understand all the effort and pain of having to do the research on the ingredients and quality of supplements you are looking to purchase. That is why we invented a blockchain based software platform to track and audit the production process and share all the information about the quality of ingredients, processes used, and related scientific research with consumers.

Look for products labeled with Research Blocks’ quality mark and simply scan the code with your phone to access all the related data. Additionally, you can view our list of trusted manufacturers for each industry on our website. At Research Blocks, we know the future of consumables is rooted in transparency and how important your health is to you. This is why we are doing what we can to ensure quality and safe products enter the market.
