Dietary Supplements._

An unregulated industry desperately in need of transformation

Repairing a Broken Ecosystem._

The FDA is not authorized to review dietary supplements before they go to market. This means dietary supplements are safe until proven to be unsafe and the general population is used as guinea pigs. Dietary supplements are not pulled from the shelves until something major happens, which is not uncommon. It has also been found that 4 out of 5 dietary supplements do not contain any of the herbs listed on their labels and some have even been found to contain germs, pesticides, and toxic heavy metals.

With all these problems in the industry, how can honest dietary supplement manufacturers stand out and how do consumers know which dietary supplement brands to trust?

Quality, Transparency, Integrity._

Research Blocks provides a software platform for dietary supplement manufacturers to track and timestamp their production process and share that process with consumers so dietary supplements can be more transparent and credible. By utilizing a blockchain ledger system, encrypted and publicly stored time-stamped data can be shared with consumers and all participants of the supply chain. Thus eliminating fraud and ensuring only quality products are produced.

Once a product is produced and monitored by Research Blocks’ software, a QR code is generated and placed on the final product. Customers can scan this QR code and determine the source and quality of the ingredients, an overview of the manufacturing process along with the quality checks implemented, and related scientific research. This will allow consumers to know that not only was a quality product produced but it will also have the intended results.
