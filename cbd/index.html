Cannabidiol._

Ensuring the quality of a growing treatment option

The Rush to Market._

Cannabidiol (CBD) is a compound isolated from the Cannabis Sativa (Hemp) plant. Based on its terpene content and chemical composition, CBD can be used to treat a wide range of ailments such as chronic pain, anxiety, and inflammation.

CBD is becoming widely accepted and more available, causing hundreds of companies to open up and produce their own CBD oil. However, how do you know which companies produce quality oils and have the effect that they claim?

Monitoring Every Step._

Research Blocks software allows CBD manufacturers to upload all Certificates of Analyses (CoAs) through the production cycle and store them on one database.

Additionally, Research Blocks monitors the processes used during extraction and post-extraction to produce the final product. This allows manufacturers to streamline their production process and easily document all required paperwork.

Transparency In Every Bottle._

Once a CBD product is complete, a QR code is generated and placed on the bottle. The QR code will share all the CoAs, processes used during production, and scientific research related to the CBD and terpene content of the product.

This way consumers will know that only quality ingredients were used, what exact processes were used to produce the final chemical composition, and be able to see verified claims about the product.
